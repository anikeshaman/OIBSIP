About us

Our main Signature dish is Matka meat & Chicken
Champaran meat, also known as ahuna, handi meat or batlohi, is a dish with its root from Champaran,a district of Bihar. Meat is marinated in a mix of mustard oil and ghee, garlic, onions, ginger with the paste of spices. The mouth of the handi (earthenware pot) is sealed with kneaded flour.
